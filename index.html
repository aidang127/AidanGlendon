Internship Experience

Reedpop

As a summer intern at Reedpop, I had many different responsibilities. I learned the program Salesforce and used it to book artists for artist alley for both NYCC and KCC. I even taught others around the office to use the software. I monitored different show social media accounts and took notes on fan interaction. I sent out retailer packages for local stores to sell badges to upcoming comic cons, and even got hands on experience working at Book Expo at the Jacob Javits Convention Center, helping attendees from behind an info desk. It was all an exciting experience for which I am very grateful.
